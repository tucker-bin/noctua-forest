We use Stripe Connect to pay Curators. Go to your Account → Earnings and click “Set up payouts.” Stripe will guide you through onboarding (tax/KYC). Once enabled, monthly earnings are paid out to your connected account.

All Night Owl members automatically participate in our curator program. You earn commissions when readers purchase books through your shared lists. Commission rates are tiered based on monthly sales volume:
  • Tier 1: 1% (0-2 books/month)
  • Tier 2: 2% (3-14 books/month)
  • Tier 3: 3% (15-99 books/month)
  • Tier 4: 3.5% (100+ books/month)
Tiers are calculated monthly and reset each month. Higher tiers mean higher earnings on every sale you generate.
Commission tiers are based on the number of books sold through your shared lists each month. Your tier resets on the 1st of every month. For example, if you sell 5 books in January, you'll earn 2% commission on all sales that month. If you sell 20 books in February, you'll earn 3% commission on all sales that month. The more books you help sell, the higher your commission rate becomes.
