How it works

Creators (you)

  • Create custom reading lists featuring books you love
  • Share links or QR codes anywhere (social, streams, in‑person)
  • Earn commissions on qualifying purchases (7.5% Bookshop.org, 2.5% Amazon)
  • Optionally submit your own books to be featured in Noctua Forest for free; premium staff review is optional

Readers

  • Discover books through your lists and recommendations
  • Leave authentic "Reader Reviews" using a Forest Name
  • Support creators and Noctua Forest at no extra cost

Simple steps

  1. Apply and get approved
  2. Generate your creator code and build your first list
  3. Share your links and start earning

Authenticity is key — recommend what you genuinely enjoy and tell readers who will love it.

Creator FAQ

How are commissions tracked?

Use your creator code or share links from the dashboard. When a reader buys through your links, we attribute the sale to you.

When are payouts sent?

Payouts are issued on a regular schedule after the return window has passed. You can view pending amounts in your dashboard.

What kind of content performs best?

Short, honest recommendations work well. Share why a book matters to you and who will enjoy it. Authenticity helps readers decide.

How do I share my links?

Copy your site link or a deep book link with your code from the dashboard. Post it in your caption or profile as needed.

Can I change my Forest Name later?

Yes. You can update your Forest Name in your dashboard. If a name is taken, choose another available option.

Do I have to feature certain books?

No. You choose the books you share. We encourage you to recommend titles you genuinely enjoy.
