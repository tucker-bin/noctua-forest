Content Policy

At Noctua Forest, we aim to foster meaningful discussions about books while maintaining a respectful and inclusive environment. This policy outlines our guidelines for user-generated content, including reviews, comments, and submissions.

General Guidelines

  • Be respectful and constructive in your interactions
  • Focus on the content of books rather than personal attacks
  • Maintain authenticity in your reviews and recommendations
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property rights
  • Protect privacy and personal information

Prohibited Content

The following types of content are not allowed:

  • Hate speech, discrimination, or harassment
  • Explicit or adult content
  • Spam, scams, or misleading information
  • Unauthorized copyright material
  • Personal attacks or bullying
  • Off-topic or irrelevant content
  • Promotional content without proper disclosure

Review Guidelines

  • Focus on your genuine experience with the book
  • Provide constructive criticism when applicable
  • Avoid spoilers without proper warnings
  • Disclose any potential conflicts of interest
  • Respect the creative work of authors

Affiliate Content

When sharing affiliate links or sponsored content:

  • Clearly disclose affiliate relationships
  • Maintain authenticity in recommendations
  • Follow FTC guidelines for endorsements
  • Respect platform-specific affiliate policies

Enforcement

We reserve the right to:

  • Remove content that violates these guidelines
  • Suspend or terminate accounts for repeated violations
  • Modify this policy as needed to maintain community standards

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or need to report a violation, please contact us.
