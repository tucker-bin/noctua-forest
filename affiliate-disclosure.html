Affiliate Disclosure

Our Affiliate Partnerships

Noctua Forest participates in various affiliate programs to support our community and platform development. When you purchase books through our affiliate links, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

Amazon Associates Program

We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for us to earn fees by linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.

When you click on book cover images or purchase links on our site, you may be directed to Amazon where your purchase supports our community through small referral fees.

Transparency and Trust

We believe in complete transparency about our affiliate relationships. This disclosure is prominently displayed in our footer and on relevant pages throughout the site.

Our affiliate partnerships do not influence our book recommendations, reviews, or community content. We only recommend books that our community genuinely enjoys and finds valuable.

Community Benefits

The commissions we earn through affiliate partnerships help us:

  • Maintain and improve our platform infrastructure
  • Support community features and development
  • Provide commission opportunities for Night Owl curators
  • Keep our core services free for all users

Your Privacy

When you use our affiliate links, we do not collect any personal information about your purchases. The affiliate programs may use cookies to track referrals, but this is standard practice and helps ensure proper attribution for our community.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate partnerships or how they work, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're committed to maintaining your trust and being completely transparent about our business relationships.
