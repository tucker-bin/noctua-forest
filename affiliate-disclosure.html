Curator Commission Disclosure

Our Curator Commission Program

Noctua Forest operates a curator commission program where all users can earn commissions by sharing book recommendations. When someone purchases books through your shared links, you earn a commission at no additional cost to the buyer.

Amazon Associates Program

We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, which enables our curators to earn commissions by linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites when sharing book recommendations.

When you click on book cover images or purchase links on our site, you may be directed to Amazon where your purchase supports our community through small referral fees.

Transparency and Trust

We believe in complete transparency about our curator commission program. This disclosure is prominently displayed in our footer and on relevant pages throughout the site.

Our commission program does not influence our book recommendations, reviews, or community content. We only recommend books that our community genuinely enjoys and finds valuable.

Community Benefits

The commissions earned through our curator program help us:

  • Maintain and improve our platform infrastructure
  • Support community features and development
  • Provide commission opportunities for Night Owl curators
  • Keep our core services free for all users

Your Privacy

When you use curator links, we do not collect any personal information about your purchases. The commission programs may use cookies to track referrals, but this is standard practice and helps ensure proper commission attribution to our curators.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our curator commission program or how it works, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're committed to maintaining your trust and being completely transparent about our business relationships.
